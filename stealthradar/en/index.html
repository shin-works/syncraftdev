App Features

GPS App that Transforms Daily Life into Adventure

Transform familiar places in the real world into adventure fields. Endless ways to play make both adults and children excited to go on adventures.

New discoveries and excitement await you.

Commander Mode

Place pins on the map and send encrypted "location data" and "missions" to searchers (Free version: 1 pin, Paid version: up to 8 pins). Set adventure destinations for your team

Searcher Mode

Analyze encrypted links received from commanders and search for targets using radar. Missions activate when approaching destinations. Enjoy real-world exploration
