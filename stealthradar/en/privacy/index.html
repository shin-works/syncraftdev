PRIVACY POLICY

STEALTH RADAR (hereinafter "App") has established the following policy regarding the handling of location information and usage data to ensure that all users, including children, can enjoy safe and secure experiences.

We strive to provide safe and secure experiences for all users, including children.

1. Information We Collect

The information used by this App is limited to the following:

Location Information Used for adventure mission determination. Location information is processed only on the device and is never stored or transmitted to external servers.

Usage Data (Anonymous) We may collect crash information and usage statistics anonymously for app improvement and bug fixes. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, etc.).

2. Purpose of Information Use

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

Providing basic app functions such as adventure mission determination and map display

Analysis for app quality improvement and bug fixes

Reference for new feature development and user experience enhancement

3. Third-Party Information Sharing

This App does not provide personal information to third parties without user consent.

4. Advertising Usage (Free Version Only)

For the free version only, we may use advertising services such as Google AdMob.

Advertising IDs may be used for ad optimization, but this can be controlled through device settings.

5. Data Storage and Management

Location information is used only for real-time determination and is not stored on servers.

Anonymous usage data is managed with security considerations.

6. Children's Usage

This App is designed for children to enjoy, but please always use with a parent or guardian.

This App does not collect personal information.

Please use under parental management and consent.

7. User Choices and Rights

Users can stop location information usage at any time through device settings.

Some features may become unavailable when stopped.

8. Safety Guidelines

Using smartphones while walking is dangerous. Please stop and check safely.

Avoid using on public roads or highways, and play in safe locations.

When young children use the app, please always use with a parent or guardian.

We cannot be held responsible for accidents or issues that occur while using this app.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised as necessary.

Important changes will be announced in the app or on our official website.
