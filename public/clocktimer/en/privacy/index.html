Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

ClockTimer (hereinafter "the App") respects user privacy and handles information in accordance with the following policy.

* This policy applies to both iOS and Android versions.

1. Information We Do Not Obtain

The App does not request input of personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.

  • We do not provide account registration functionality.

2. Information We Obtain and Purpose of Use

The App may obtain the following information for quality improvement and service provision:

(1) Crash Information

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics.

Examples of information obtained

  • Device information
  • OS version
  • Crash logs
  • Technical information regarding usage

Purpose of use

  • Identifying defects
  • Improving app quality

This information is collected anonymously, and we do not identify individuals.

(2) Advertising-Related Information

The App uses Google AdMob. The following information may be obtained for ad delivery optimization:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Usage-related information

This information is managed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

(3) Billing-Related Information

The App uses RevenueCat for subscription management.

  • Payments are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • We do not obtain credit card information.
  • Anonymous identifiers may be used for purchase status verification.

3. Data Storage

Data such as tasks and habit records are stored on the user's device.

We currently do not support cloud sync or backup features.

Data may be lost in the following cases:

  • Uninstalling the app
  • Changing devices
  • Device reset
  • OS or storage malfunctions

Data management is the responsibility of the user.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • RevenueCat

Please refer to each service's privacy policy for their information handling practices.

5. Use by Minors

The App is not intended for any specific age group.

  • Users under 13 years of age should use the App with parental consent.

6. Information Management and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to prevent unauthorized access, leakage, tampering, and the like for information we obtain.

7. User Rights (Limited)

For information we directly manage, we will respond in a reasonable manner when disclosure, correction, deletion, or similar actions are possible under applicable law.

For information managed by third-party services (Google, Apple, RevenueCat, etc.), please contact each service in accordance with their respective policies.

8. Policy Changes

This policy may be changed when necessary.

Revised policies take effect when posted in the App or on our official website.
