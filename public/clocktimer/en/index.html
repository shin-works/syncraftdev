About ClockTimer

ClockTimer is an app that brings together
a timer, habit tracking, and task organization
to help you reset your daily rhythm.

Staying consistent isn't easy.
But it helps you keep taking one steady step forward.

A simple time companion
for those who choose to start again.

Features

One More Step

Start the timer with one tap from each task. Voice guidance for remaining time.

After completion, check off. Lightly supporting your next step.

Steady Progress

Track your small steps and make progress visible. If you miss a day, you can always come back.

It's not about perfection — it's about starting again.

Stay Focused

Clean tab views keep your habits and tasks organized. Quick ToDo lets you capture ideas instantly.

So you can focus on what matters now.

ClockTimer is an app with a focus-enhancing timer, a habit tracker for recording routines, and tab-based task management for easy organization.

Use it as a Pomodoro timer for work or study, and build daily habits and task management without pressure.

Currently, the app's display language supports Japanese and English, with plans to expand support gradually.

Voice guidance is provided in recorded voice for Japanese and English. For other languages, TTS (text-to-speech) is available based on your device's language settings.

* TTS uses the device's voice engine, so voice quality and reading accuracy depend on device environment.

Currently, data is stored on your device. Data is not transferred when you change devices or delete the app.
