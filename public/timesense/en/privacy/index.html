TimeSense Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2025

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

TimeSense ("the App") respects user privacy and handles personal information in accordance with the policy below.

This page includes information for both the iOS and Android versions.

Please review the section relevant to the platform you are using.

Some features of the App and the external services it uses may vary depending on the platform or operating system.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.

However, the following information may be stored locally on the user's device:

  • App settings, such as timer settings and selected voices
  • Routine settings
  • Usage records, including stamp card history
  • Premium usage status

This information is primarily stored on the user's device.

In the Android version, anonymized statistical data may be sent to external services for usage analysis and app improvement.

2. Use of Third-Party Services

■ iOS Version

The iOS version does not use the following external services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Firebase Analytics (usage analytics)

In the iOS version, the app operates primarily on the device and does not send personal information or usage data externally.

■ Android Version

The Android version may use the following third-party services for providing and improving the App:

● Google AdMob (Advertising)

The Android version may display banner advertisements using Google AdMob.

The App is submitted to Google Play as a "Designed for Families" app, and advertisements are delivered in compliance with Google's Families policies.

We make reasonable efforts to avoid inappropriate advertisements being shown.

● Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)

The Android version may use Firebase Analytics to improve the App and understand usage trends.

Information collected by Firebase Analytics is anonymized statistical data and does not directly identify individuals.

Analytics data is sent only when the user has consented, such as on first launch.

This setting can be changed at any time from within the App's settings.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases through Google Play or the App Store.

Payment processing is handled securely by each store, and the App does not store or collect payment information such as credit card details.

Purchase information and premium features are tied to the account on the platform where the purchase was made, either Google Play or the App Store.

Purchase status is not shared between the iOS and Android versions.

4. Privacy Policies of External Services

Please refer to the following for more information about each service:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be changed as needed.

Any changes become effective when posted in the App or on our website.
