TimeSense Privacy Policy

TimeSense ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.

Note: This page includes information for both iOS and Android versions. Please refer to the relevant sections according to your device.

Note: Certain features and use of third-party services may vary depending on platform-specific policies or technical limitations.

1. Information Collected

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (such as names or email addresses).

However, the following information may be stored locally on your device:

  • App settings (e.g., timer preferences, selected sounds)
  • Premium usage status (e.g., purchases or ad-based unlocks)

This information is stored solely on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.
